About HotelsNearMeInKerala.com

Kerala's dedicated hotel booking platform — helping travellers find the perfect stay across God's Own Country since 2024.

Who We Are

HotelsNearMeInKerala.com is a Kerala-focused travel booking platform built to make it easy for travellers — from India and around the world — to find and book the perfect accommodation in Kerala.

We specialize in listing hotels, luxury resorts, budget hotels, beach resorts, hill station stays, traditional homestays, and the iconic Kerala houseboats — all in one place.

Our platform is built and managed from Kerala, by people who know Kerala. We work directly with property owners across all 14 districts to bring you accurate listings, real photos, and the best available prices.
