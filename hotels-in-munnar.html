Why Stay in Munnar?

Munnar is Kerala's premier hill station destination — a stunning landscape of rolling tea plantations, mist-covered peaks, and cool mountain air. At 1,600 metres above sea level, it offers relief from Kerala's coastal heat and some of the most dramatic natural scenery in South India.

From luxury tea garden resorts with valley views to cosy mountain homestays, Munnar has accommodation for every budget. Key attractions include Eravikulam National Park (home to the Nilgiri Tahr), Top Station viewpoint, Attukal Waterfalls, and the famous rolling tea estates of Kolukkumalai.
