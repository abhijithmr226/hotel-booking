Kerala's Best Resorts

Kerala is renowned globally for its world-class resort experiences. Whether it's a Ayurveda spa retreat on the Alappuzha backwaters, a luxury tea estate villa in Munnar, a clifftop boutique resort in Varkala, or a beach resort in Kovalam — Kerala's resorts set the global benchmark for eco-luxury and wellness tourism.

Many of Kerala's resorts offer all-inclusive Ayurveda packages, yoga retreats, backwater houseboat cruises, and wildlife safaris as add-ons. All resorts listed here are verified with direct contact information.

FAQs — Resorts in Kerala

Common questions about booking resorts in Kerala

The best beach resorts are in Kovalam (Thiruvananthapuram), Varkala, Cherai Beach (Kochi), Kappad Beach (Kozhikode) and Bekal (Kasaragod). Kovalam and Varkala are most popular for international tourists.

Kerala is world-famous for authentic Ayurveda resorts. Top destinations include Kumarakom and Alappuzha for backwater Ayurveda retreats, Thrissur for heritage centres, and Thiruvananthapuram for traditional Panchakarma therapy.

For couples, the most romantic resorts are backwater villas in Kumarakom, clifftop boutique properties in Varkala, treehouse resorts in Wayanad, and hillside tea estate resorts in Munnar.
