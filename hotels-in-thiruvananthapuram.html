Why Stay in Thiruvananthapuram?

Thiruvananthapuram (Trivandrum) is Kerala's capital and one of India's most historically rich cities. Home to the world-famous Padmanabhaswamy Temple, the magnificent Napier Museum, and Trivandrum Zoo, the city blends ancient heritage with modern amenities. It's also the gateway to Kovalam and Varkala beaches.

The city has an excellent mix of business hotels near the Technopark IT hub, heritage properties near the temple, and luxury beach resorts in Kovalam just 14 km away.
