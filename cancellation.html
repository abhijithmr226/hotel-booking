Cancellation Policy

Understanding refunds and cancellations for your Kerala hotel booking

Last Updated: June 2026

At HotelsNearMeInKerala.com, we understand that travel plans can change. This policy explains how cancellations and refunds are handled for bookings made through our platform.

Important Note

Cancellation policies may vary by property. The specific policy for your booking is shown on the hotel listing page and in your booking confirmation email. This page outlines our standard platform policy.

Standard Cancellation Windows

Cancellation TimingRefund AmountProcessing Time

How to Cancel a Booking

  • Log into your account and go to My Bookings
  • Select the booking you want to cancel and click Cancel Booking
  • Confirm the cancellation — you will receive an email confirmation
  • Refunds are processed to your original payment method

If you are unable to cancel via your account, please contact us immediately with your booking ID.

Refund Processing

  • Refunds are credited to the original payment method only
  • Processing times depend on your bank or card provider (typically 5–10 business days)
  • We will send a refund confirmation email once the refund has been initiated

Non-Refundable Bookings

Some properties offer discounted non-refundable rates. These bookings are clearly marked as "Non-Refundable" at the time of booking and in your confirmation. No refund will be issued for cancellations of non-refundable bookings.

Date Changes / Modifications

To modify your check-in or check-out dates, please contact us at least 48 hours before your original check-in date. Date changes are subject to availability and the hotel's approval. Price differences may apply.

Force Majeure

In exceptional circumstances (natural disasters, government travel bans, etc.), we will work with our hotel partners to offer flexible cancellation or rescheduling options. Please contact us as soon as possible in such cases.

Hotel-Specific Policies

Individual properties may have stricter cancellation policies, particularly during peak seasons (Christmas, New Year, Onam, school holidays). Always check the specific hotel policy on the listing page before booking.
