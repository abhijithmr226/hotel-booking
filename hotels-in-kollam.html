Why Stay in Kollam?

Kollam (formerly Quilon) is one of Kerala's oldest port cities, famous for Ashtamudi Lake — the second-largest wetland in Kerala. The famous Alappuzha-Kollam boat cruise, one of India's longest backwater routes, starts from Kollam Boat Jetty. The city also has beautiful beaches at Thangassery, a historic lighthouse, and excellent seafood cuisine.

From lakeside houseboat resorts to budget city hotels, Kollam offers excellent value for money compared to more touristy destinations like Kochi or Alappuzha.
